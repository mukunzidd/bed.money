    Why do you choose to use an expensive Telecom network for your mobile money transcations whne there's a cheaper alternative, in this case MTN versus Airtel?

    Airtel is the best network compared to MTN. The problem is that we're already used to using MTN services. Our firends, our families, our bosses, our clients all use MTN on the daily. It's hard to call someone who won't call you back because they are using another network. So, if you look at the cost-effectiveness, Airtel is the best. Even the internet is very fast.

    On the other hand, Airtel has a lot of work to do even if they are doing some advertisement. I think that the reason why we're sticking to MTN is that our people, our friends are on MTN. So, it's hard to tell someone: "Do you have Airtel? I want to send you some money." But as people buy Airtel for internet, they'll end up using it for other services too, like SMS and calling even mobile money.
